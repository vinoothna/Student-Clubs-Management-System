ALL ABOUT IT!

CREWS - Student Club Management System is an application used by students, faculty and staff to schedule and manage events of student clubs at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sri City. It enables club administrators to interact with the participants in their respective clubs. All the announcements, polls etc. are handled easily.

Made under the guidance of Uma Garimella, Dr. Subramaniam and Nagesh Kolagani.
